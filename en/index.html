SIMBa is a youth mathematics seminar organized by graduate students of the Barcelona area. It is aimed towards graduate and last course undergraduate students. Our goals are divulging the knowledge from different branches of mathematics for those interested and promote networking between the attendants.

This seminar is backed by the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science at Universitat de Barcelona, Faculty of Mathematics and Statistics at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, the Department of Mathematics from Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, CRM, IMUB and BGSMath.
