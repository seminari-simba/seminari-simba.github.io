Do you want to give a talk?

Fill in the following form: SIMBa Form

The talks have a time limit of 40 minutes where the language can be either Catalan, Spanish or English, although the latter is preferred. Keep in mind that the audience you are going to have ranges from last-year undergraduate students to doctoral students, therefore they will be informative talks. The talks are given via Zoom, so we strongly recommend the use of slides.

If you want to receive information about the next seminars, register to our mailing list via this link: SIMBa Subscription.

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking on the link that will arrive to the confirmation email.
